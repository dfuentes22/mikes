Mike's Music Shop

A Full-service Electric & Acoustic Guitar & Bass Shop

Mike's Music Shop® ("Mikes-Music-Shop.com") has its beginnings in 1975, starting primarily as a Bluegrass stringed instrument shop. From the late 1970's we started to see the need for more of a service and consumer oriented music store—one which carried the parts and accessories in stock that people needed after purchasing their guitar, bass, amp, etc. (be it from us or a competitor). Over the years, there have been many changes in the size and location of Mike's Music Shop. This growth is attributed to increased business, which is due to giving our customers the service & care they deserve and have come to expect. As a result, Mikes-Music-Shop.com has evolved into one of the regions premier, full service electric and acoustic guitar & bass shops. We at Mike's Music Shop look forward to meeting you and hope you enjoy our website. For our phone number, business hours, address or traveling directions, just click on the "Contacts" button. Enjoy!
