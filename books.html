Books

Welcome to our "Books" section! We have one of the largest selections—over 1000 titles in stock—for guitar and bass players on the East Coast; from major publishers like Music Sales Corp.® (Oak/Amsco Publications®), Hal Leonard® Corp./Cherry Lane Music®, Warner Bros. Publications®/CPP®/Belwin Inc.®, Koala Publications®, Mel Bay Publications® and Creative Concepts Publishing Corp.® Our selection is wide and varied. We carry beginning to advanced instruction books, specialized stylistic method books for Rock, Jazz, Pop, Blues, Bebop, Irish/Celtic, Funk, Country, Classical, Finger Style, Thrash, Reggae, Punk, Shred, Heavy Metal, Flat Picking, Bluegrass, Folk, Rockabilly, Swing, Alternative and much, MUCH, more! We carry all the major artists' tab books—from Chet Atkins to Frank Zappa—and a large selection of tech., repair, historical and instrument identification publications (not to mention TONS of guitar-related magazines!). And, if we don't have it in-stock—we will order it. Stop in and check us out! If you would like to learn more about the products we carry, please visit our "Links" page—where you will find direct hyperlinks to all our suppliers, publishers and manufacturers. See ya!
