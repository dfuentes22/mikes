Amps

Hi and welcome to the "Amps" section of our Web site. First let me say that we do not carry amp heads and speaker cabinets. We have always preferred to carry small combo guitar and bass amps—guitar amps from 10 to 120 watts and bass amps from 10 to 200 watts. We offer amps from world famous manufacturers such as Marshall®, Kustom®, Pig Nose®, Roland® and more! If you would like to learn more about the products we carry, please visit our "Links" page—where you will find direct hyperlinks to all our suppliers, publishers and manufacturers. See ya!

Mike Z. (Owner/Operator)
